## Q3.1

See [q3_1.cpp](./ch3/q3_1.cpp). I wanted to avoid a couple of key things with my solution:

1. Having an upper limit to the size of any of the stacks stored in the array. For example, if we had a 99-element array holding three stacks, I didn't want each stack to have a max size of 33 elements.

2. Needing to copy or move elements if any one stack grew until it reached another stack.
